Dry Cleaning & Laundry Franchising

About Tide Cleaners

The mission at Tide Cleaners is to make caring for clothes so perfect and simple, that peoples’ lives don’t have to revolve around their laundry. Tide Cleaners offers a variety of dry cleaning and laundry services for guests using convenient access points. Convenience, quality and care are the key components that make Tide Cleaners a success.

Who We Are

Launched in 2008, Tide Cleaners is part of the Procter & Gamble Company (P&G) headquartered in Cincinnati, Ohio. P&G has over 185 years of history with 65 brands and operates in approximately 70 countries. As a trusted brand for more than 75 years, Tide® is America’s number-one laundry detergent and the backbone of Tide Cleaners.

The Tide Cleaners Experience

Tide Cleaners makes it easy and convenient for busy customers to take care of their laundry. Tide was born from the belief that the future of laundry could be different than the present. A belief in "life, not laundry" for the customer.

Locations

Tide Cleaners is a blend of innovation and service that’s changing the dry cleaning experience for good. From our state-of-the-art building design to our customer-centric amenities and service, we cater to today’s on-the-go lifestyle. A clean, bright look, inside and out, along with valet service and 24/7 access to clothes, we deliver a quality and convenient experience to customers with each visit.

The Care That Keeps Customers Coming Back

Tide Cleaners exclusively uses Tide Complete Care™ Hygienic Clean, a new detergent developed by a team with more than 100 years of research and development experience. The four-step cleaning experience combines our Tide Diamond Formula, color-safe bleach, Downy fabric conditioner, and proprietary whiteness-enhancing technology to deliver the cleanest clean ever.

Awards And Recognition

Tide Cleaners is repeatedly recognized for its success and quality. We are also proudly recognized for our national sponsorships. Tide Cleaners is proud to be the official sponsor of the National Football League.
