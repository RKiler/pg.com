P&G Singapore Innovation Center (SgIC)

The P&G Singapore innovation center (SgIC) is one of the largest private research facilities in Singapore. Our passion is end-to-end consumer goods innovation, enabled by advanced capabilities. From robotics to virtual reality, chemistry to consumer understanding and biology to big data, the SgIC is the home of #TheFutureMakers.
