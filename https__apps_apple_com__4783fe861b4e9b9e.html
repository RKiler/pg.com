Description

Meet Pampers Club: the parenting app that helps new parents earn back with every diaper change. Use the app to catch rewards & deals on baby care products as well as to keep track of your baby’s development!


The baby app that helps you earn back

Hundreds of thousands of parents love Pampers Club, and there’s good reason for that: the savings, access to exclusive promotions, baby care content, and more. Best of all, it’s free to use!

Just as important, with the parenting app, new parents get the chance to earn Pampers Cash with each diaper code they scan, which they can use to redeem terrific Pampers rewards!

How does it work? Pampers Club is a loyalty program available via the app, which you can download for free in the Apple store. To start earning Pampers Cash, use the app to scan codes on the inside of each diaper pack.

With enough Pampers Cash, you can earn rewards like free diapers*, wipes, coupons, and discounts. Let each diaper change work for you!


Welcome to the Club!

Here’s a brief overview of what you’ll be able to take advantage of when you join Pampers Club:

Savings
• Each diaper pack contains a code that you can scan to earn Pampers Cash!

Rewards
• From free diapers* to coupons and discounts, there are useful rewards waiting for you in our catalog.

Parenting Tools
• What to expect in the first year and beyond? We’ve got a library of interactive tools at your disposal to help you track your baby’s progress.

Expert-led Content
• Looking for guidance on each baby milestone? That’s what you’ll find here.


Parenting tools for you and your baby

Another useful tool that you’ll find in the Pampers Club app is our handy diaper size tracker. Using the correct diaper size can be a difference-maker when it comes to preventing leaks and blowouts. We want to help you find the exact right fit for your baby, and we know that it can be tricky to do that when your baby’s growing so quickly. That’s where our diaper size tracking tool can come in handy!

Your Pampers Club parenting journey

From pregnancy to newborn feeding to potty training and each baby milestone beyond, Pampers Club is here for you. In the app, you’ll find all sorts of useful baby care and parenting content that’s tailored to every step along the parenting journey—whether you’re a new parent or an experienced one!

Here's a taste of what you can find in the app:
• Childbirth Education
• Baby Development
• Milestone Checklist
• Month-by-month tips
• Diapering
• Feeding

There’s also fun stuff like baby quizzes, birthday facts, and personal stories from other parents. Plus, you’ll find useful tools like a baby name generator and a growth chart calculator.

Need help with baby-shopping? When you’re coming up with your baby essentials checklist, we’ve got articles that can offer useful insight into a variety of products useful at any stage in your baby’s progress, from pregnancy to the things you might ask for at your baby shower to the best gifts to tools that can help with your baby’s sleep and beyond.


Whether you’re a first-time parent looking for advice on what to expect in the first year or an experienced parent who’s been through it all, Pampers Club is with you through each step and diaper change!

P&G does not like to lose

Out of all the major companies, Procter & Gamble is the stingiest. Their products are on the higher end, but for the most part remain comparable to its counterparts, but unlike other companies, Procter & Gamble does not like to let the consumer win. When they put out a coupon for one of their products, it is for a very low amount ($.50 off 1, $1.25 off 3, ect.) Whereas other companies will put out a few low amount coupons as well as some high value ones ($3.50 off 2, $10 off 3, $2 off 1).

I’m not saying I don’t appreciate the chance to save or get back some money for something that I don’t have a choice in purchasing (lol). Just saying P&G makes you work harder then others to save less. Scanning and TYPING in codes and honestly the hardest part is remembering to do it and hoping my husband doesn’t throw out packaging before I get to it (I’ve learned to put codes into app as soon as I purchase the items)! I guess my point is… give us something unexpected. Put a little excitement in our lives. 🤣🤣🤣

Other then the financial aspect, the app is great. It’s educational, and also gives great tips!
