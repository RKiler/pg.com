Field force Automated

It provides fast and efficient methodology with the objective to automate research process, from digitizing your outdoor activities, customized research, sales distribution to automating retail & merchandizing tracking and mystery shopping – all at your fingertips

Activations Automated

WS-Provision gives you the visibility of your field force working on-ground. Through this application you can plan, strategize, optimize, manage and monitor your field/sales team. WS-Provision equips you to eradicate the process of cumbersome paper-handling and eliminates the time lag making the process real-time, cost saving and time saving. WS –Provision validates the Quality Assurance of your investment.

Retail and Merchandizing Audits

WS-Provision offers you automated solution for your retail and merchandizing tracking to a retail snapshot of the presence of your product. This automated solution enables you to keep an eye on all of the retail and merchandizing activities from checking the availability and visibility of your brand to providing you with the real time images of all of your merchandising activities. WS-Provision helps you to run audit studies with single as well as multiple categories or brands. It places utmost importance on driving accountability for your retail and merchandizing business by automatically geo-tagging/geo-fencing and time-stamping the activities. Moreover, with the real-time images of merchandizing activities it keep you in-the-know about your competitors as well.

Research Digitized

WS-Provision has automated the research data collection along with different QA check to substantiate the data. This state of the art application helps us transform the traditional research approach – converting the unwieldy and unnecessarily complex research process to digital data collection and that too in real time.

Mobile Mystery Shoppers

Our tech solution helps you to monitor your sales and activation staff adding the consumer perspective through mobile mystery shopper. Get real time results of the mystery shopping with a replicated profile of respondent for your brand in real time, without lag and geo tagged to your location. Mobile mystery shopper helps the shopper rate the experience in real time and without any time lag, simultaneously enabling the client getting real time view of consumer perspective.
