Unattainable Pampers Rewards

Pampers Club offers points for each bag of diapers you buy. Simply scan the bar code on each bag to collect points toward free diapers and other free things. Great. Now to the funny part. I’ve been scanning the barcodes since our daughter started wearing diapers, and in 19 months the points don’t even add up to $3 off on a new box of diapers. Clearly this is a “make me feel good”, and otherwise useless, points app that will never get you anything unless your child keeps wearing diapers until grade 12. LOL. I wonder if any other parents ever noticed this or if it is just me.
