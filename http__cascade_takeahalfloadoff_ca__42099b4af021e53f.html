8 DISHES IS ALL IT TAKES.

8 dishes is all it takes to save water with your dishwasher over handwashing. So, run ‘em if you got ‘em! And don’t forget that with Cascade Platinum, no pre-rinse needed. 

#3: Dishwashers waste more energy.

HANDWASHING TAKES 4X THE ENERGY. 

A new Energy Star certified dishwasher uses about ¼ of the energy used when washing dishes by hand. 

#4: Handwashing saves water.

DISHWASHERS SAVE 378 LITRES A WEEK.

Your dishwasher saves 378 litres of water a week, when you use it every night instead of handwashing. That’s over 19,600 litres in a year. Or over 87 bathtubs of water you could be saving.

You save almost 10 days of personal time in a year by letting your dishwasher with Cascade do the work over handwashing. What could you do with 30 extra minutes a day?
