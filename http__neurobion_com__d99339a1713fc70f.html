Signs and Symptoms of Nerve Damage

You might be unaware that some minor discomforts like tingling on the feet, tingly hands, pins and needles or burning sensations—which are often ignored—could in fact be signs of nerve damage or Neuropathy.

What is Nerve damage?

Nerve damage is the medical term for a condition where your peripheral nerves are damaged due to B vitamin deficiency. These are the nerves that carry signals between your brain and your spinal cord (the central nervous system) and the rest of your body.
