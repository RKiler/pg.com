Monkeypox Info

*Microban 24 products are on EPA List N as disinfectants for use against SARS-CoV-2 (the virus that causes COVID-19). Follow directions for disinfecting shown on product label. Microban Sanitizing Spray and Multi-Purpose Cleaner are initially effective against Respiratory Syncytial Virus [RSV], Human Coronavirus, Influenza A H1N1 virus. Microban Bathroom Cleaner is initially effective against Rhinovirus, Respiratory Syncytial Virus, Influenza A H1N1 virus. Microban 24 does not provide 24-hour residual virus protection.

**When used as directed, effective for 24 hours against Staphylococcus aureus & Klebsiella aerogenes bacteria. Microban 24 does not provide 24-hour residual virus protection.
